Weekly Rhythm

How the flow works

Anonymusing is intentionally slow and structured. Each week follows this loop so depth stays higher than volume.

1. Submit

A member submits a musing with context, intent, and declaration.

2. Discern

The council reviews it for clarity, constructiveness, and discussability.

3. Publish

One approved musing is released as the weekly focal reflection.

4. Respond

Members engage through structured streams, not open-ended chaos.

5. Surface

The strongest responses rise through one-account interaction signals.

6. Deepen

Responses can be forwarded to admin as sub-muse proposals for deeper follow-up.
